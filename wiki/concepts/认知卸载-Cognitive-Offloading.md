# 认知卸载 (Cognitive Offloading)

## Definition

Cognitive offloading is the use of external tools (notes, reminders, calculators, AI) to reduce internal mental load. It is a natural human strategy that can be beneficial or harmful depending on **what** is offloaded and **how**.

## Three-Tier Taxonomy

| Tier | Type             | Mechanism                                                                                    | Risk   |
| ---- | ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| 1    | **Assistive**    | External tool *cues* or *supports* cognition without replacing it (reminders, quick lookups) | Low    |
| 2    | **Substitutive** | External tool *replaces* internal processing (AI summaries, auto-generated answers)          | Medium |
| 3    | **Disruptive**   | Passive interaction becomes the norm; self-monitoring collapses; externally-driven behavior  | High   |

(Source: Frontiers 2025 — Outsourcing Cognition)

## The "Google Effect" (Sparrow et al., 2011)

People remember **where** information is stored better than **what** the information is. When we know something is saved externally, the brain deprioritizes encoding it internally. The mere act of saving reduces the motivation to mentally encode.

## The Developmental Displacement Effect

Adolescents (and learners early in a domain) are at highest risk. External tools displace internal cognitive processes "prior to achieving internal mastery." The learner never builds the capability internally because the tool always handles it.

## EEG Evidence (Kosmyna et al., 2025)

Students using LLMs to write essays showed significantly reduced brain connectivity compared to independent writers. They could not recall their own essays minutes later. This "cognitive debt" suggests neural circuits weaken through disuse.

## The Key Distinction

- **Beneficial offloading**: Offload the *mechanical* (formatting, organizing, scheduling) to free working memory for higher-order thinking
- **Harmful offloading**: Offload the *cognitive* (generating explanations, retrieving from memory, synthesizing ideas) — this is the work that builds durable understanding

## Practical Rule

> [!tip] Before saving something, ask: "Am I offloading storage or offloading thinking?" Storage offloading is fine. Thinking offloading builds cognitive debt.
